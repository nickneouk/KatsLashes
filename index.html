About Kat's Lashes

Welcome to Kat's Lashes, Southgate's premier destination for exceptional lash and brow services. Founded by Kat, a certified lash artist with over 5 years of experience, our boutique specialises in enhancing your natural beauty through expert lash lift and tint treatments.

At Kat's Lashes, we believe in the transformative power of beautiful lashes. Our meticulous attention to detail and commitment to using only premium products ensures that each client leaves feeling confident and looking their absolute best.

Located in the heart of Southgate N14, our elegant, intimate studio provides a peaceful retreat where you can relax and rejuvenate while receiving top-tier beauty services tailored to your unique features and preferences.
